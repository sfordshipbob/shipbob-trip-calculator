Storage

TRIP’s Storage Pricing

Each unique TRIP SKU will be stored in a unique location and charged monthly based on usage.

Storage Types
  • 3 storage types: pallet, large bin, and small bin
  • 1 SKU per bin or pallet location
  • Pallets can be used as pickable locations
Storage Fees
  • Fees invoiced monthly
  • Storage fees are per product (SKU)
  • Storage fees are prorated daily (as expressed as a % of the entire month on your invoice)
  • Ex: 1 Pallet (£24) location was active for 15 days out of a 30-day month. This would be prorated and charged only £12, as the location was only used for half of the month.
